Remote Property Manager

The Remote Property Manager provides a complete, detailed, and interactive site plan of your property. The ID Plans team gathers on-site information to show data points and locations for light poles, meters, HVAC units, and dozens of other property features.

Every detail is also cross-referenced to tenant space or related equipment. The data in the Remote Property Manager is categorized into twenty easy-to-read sections. Users have the ability to make changes, upload documents, and add photos within each section. The plans are truly dynamic, with changes that populate in real time, in all applicable sections, and across all users.

Space Profile

Space Profile is designed to provide all the details, photos, and drawings of your space to help you market it to prospective tenants, prepare for a construction quote, and manage it more effectively. Your Space Profile will be delivered within 10 business days in a clean, marketable, and digestible format that is housed on the cloud - all for less than the cost of most one-page, lined leased outline drawings.
