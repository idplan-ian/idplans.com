Space Profile

Space Profile is designed to provide all the details, photos, and drawings of your space to help you market it to prospective tenants, prepare for a construction quote, and manage it more effectively. Each Space Profile includes:

  • Fast Turnaround Time
  • BOMA Standard Lease Outline Drawings
  • HVAC Units and Information
  • Utility Meter Locations and Information
  • Joist & Girder Directions
  • Ceiling Deck Heights
  • Reflected Ceiling Plans
  • Restroom ADA Compliance Reports
  • Marketable Format along with Plans in CAD
  • Delivered in As Little As 10 Business Days
