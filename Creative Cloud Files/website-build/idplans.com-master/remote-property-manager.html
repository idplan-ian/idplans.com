Remote Property Manager

The Remote Property Manager allows you to maintain the accuracy and integrity of your plans as your property changes and evolves. The ID Plans team will visit your site to gather all the necessary information and create your comprehensive plans from scratch.
All it takes is five minutes to approve our team to come out and collect your site data. It’s that simple.

Save time

Tired of outdated site plans? Want to know where everything is located on your property? The Remote Property Manager will save you valuable time and allow you to focus on other aspects of running your business.

Save money

We research and collect all the information you need to manage your property, including HVAC, meters, utilities, irrigation, landscape, lighting, signage, location maps, parking, square footage, drainage, and much more.

Improve sales

Show potential investors or renters your site in an attractive, easy-to-read package and give them all the details they need to make an informed decision about their next steps.

Communicate effectively

No more meeting with contractors and vendors to help them locate HVAC units or irrigation systems. Our detailed site maps show them exactly where to go to make improvements, and you can share them from any device, at any time, from anywhere.
